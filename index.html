Privacy Policy

Devscan QR Scanner & Generator

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Devscan QR Scanner & Generator ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

✓ We do NOT store personal data on servers
✓ We do NOT track your location
✓ We do NOT share data with third parties

The App uses Google ML Kit to process QR codes and barcodes. When scanning, barcode data (which may include email addresses, names, phone numbers, or contact details embedded in QR codes) is processed ephemerally by Google Play Services on your device. This data is not stored on any server and is only used momentarily to complete the scan. Your scan history and bookmarks are stored only on your device.

Permissions Used

The App requests the following permissions to provide its core functionality:

CameraRequired to scan QR codes and barcodes.
StorageRequired to save generated QR codes to your device.
Wi-FiUsed to connect to Wi-Fi networks from scanned QR codes.

Data Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • QR/barcode scanning uses Google ML Kit, which may process scanned content (such as email addresses, names, or contact info) ephemerally through Google Play Services.
  • No personal data is sent to or stored on our servers.
  • Your scan history remains on your device only.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data.

Note: When you choose to open URLs, dial phone numbers, send emails, or connect to Wi-Fi from scanned codes, data is shared with the respective apps (browser, dialer, email client, etc.) as you direct.

Children's Privacy

This App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. The App does not require account creation or any personal data entry.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

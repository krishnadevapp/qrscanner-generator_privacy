Introduction

Welcome to QR Scanner QR Generator ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect personal data
We do NOT track your location
We do NOT share data with third parties

The App operates entirely offline and locally on your device. All QR codes you scan or generate are processed locally and are never transmitted to any server.

Permissions Used

The App requests the following permissions to provide its core functionality:

  • Camera: Required to scan QR codes and barcodes.
  • Storage: Required to save generated QR codes to your device.

Data Security

Your data security is our priority. Since all processing occurs locally on your device:

  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No cloud storage is used
  • Your scan history remains on your device
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data

Children's Privacy

This App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. The App does not require account creation or any personal data entry.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Introduction

Welcome to Devscan QR Scanner & Generator ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

✓ We do NOT collect personal data
✓ We do NOT track your location
✓ We do NOT share data with third parties

The App processes all QR codes and barcodes locally on your device. Scanned data is never transmitted to our servers. Your scan history and bookmarks are stored only on your device.

Permissions Used

The App requests the following permissions to provide its core functionality:

CameraRequired to scan QR codes and barcodes.
StorageRequired to save generated QR codes to your device.
Wi-FiUsed to connect to Wi-Fi networks from scanned QR codes.

Data Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • All QR/barcode processing happens locally on your device.
  • No personal data is sent to our servers.
  • Your scan history remains on your device only.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data.
